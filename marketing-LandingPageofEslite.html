Project Overview

Project outline / Clients’ names / Project goals / Major deliverables

Objective:

Increase user interest in Taiwanese indie music by improving the user experience of the Eslite Music landing page to boost CD sales.

Overview:

To increase CD sales of Taiwanese indie music, we conducted an analysis to reveal why Taiwanese music is mainstream in Taiwan today. The reasons are album cover design and emphasizing “Taiwanese-ness”.

To showcase the excellence of Taiwanese music on the landing page, the solutions are slideshow element, online music player, stories behind the scenes, and added jQuery effects. The sales target for Taiwan Music was achieved after the event.

User Story:

Maggie loves shopping at Eslite physical stores since shopping in Eslite is like experiencing art, design, style and culture in everyday life. She also shops on Eslite online store because the online campaigns are creative and ingenuity. However, she still prefers shopping in physical stores because she prefers to experience products in person before buying them. She is willing to pay a higher price to buy products from Eslite. Because she has a strong relationship with Eslite and she believes that branded goods are of higher quality and therefore are worth a higher price. However, it is still good to have more detailed information and values of the products. This could help her to know what makes the product so special, and how it helps to solve her problem.

Starting the Design

Include problems, solutions, flowcharts, wireframes to understand the beginning of a design

Problem:

How to increase sales accurately and effectively.

This landing page focuses on amplifying the value of the product through design. The first phase of the project aims to figure out why the younger generation is paying more and more attention to Taiwanese music. The reasons are handled as follows:

  • Album Cover Design: Taiwanese music is known for its admirable, unique and strong aesthetic visual of the album cover designs
  • Emphasize “Taiwanese-ness”: Most Taiwanese indie bands adapt Taiwanese folk songs and use traditional instruments in their songs, or write the lyrical content of their albums in Taiwanese.

Solutions:

To highlight the unique selling points of Taiwanese music, the following solutions are given:

Slideshow Element: 

Slideshow element to view more album cover images, users can click the cover album to view more details of album covers design

Online Music Player:

Embedded online music player that allows users to list music

Behind-the-scenes:

Telling behind-the-scenes stories about the band's stated goals and vision (for example, some bands are on a mission to bring ancient history and mythology through their music to build awareness of Taiwanese mythology and tragic events in the country's history)

JQuery Effects:

Apply media plug-ins, and jQuery effects to enhance user experience, and highlight the advantages of Taiwanese music

UI Design:

The main visual of this landing page is designed to convey the value of Taiwanese music to users. In order to capture the user’s attention, an eye-catching key visual was designed as an important part of this landing page. Additionally, jQuery effects and animated graphics are used to showcase the physical product and explain the song and album concepts in more detail.

Colour:

To make users feel calm, quiet, and then connect to Taiwanese music, we chose a pastel orange colour to remind people to stay calm.

Typography:

An old-style serif typeface “Garamond” was applied for readability and the elegant style of Taiwanese music.
